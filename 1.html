WHAT WE ARE

M.E.T School ,Supa .The school is affiliated to the State Board of Secondary Education ,Pune. The school has excellent infrastructure with well equipped Science, Mathematics ,Computer Labs and library .Well qualified ,trained and dedicated staff support and co-ordinates with the management in fulfilling the needs of the students.

The medium of instruction is English .The subjects taught are English ,Hindi, Mathematics,GeneralScience, History&Civics,Geography and Computer .Believing in the idea that a healthy mind nestles in a healthy body ,the school takes care of the holistic development of the child by providing ample opportunites for co-curricular activities .

Our Glorious Dreams

It is our dream that each child of M.E.T School will not only be prosperous but also all rounded.
